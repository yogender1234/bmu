Welcome to BMU, Haryana

A Decade of Excellence in Higher Education

Baba Mastnath University, Rohtak, is a private self-financing university established in 2012 under the Haryana Private Universities Act, 2006. The university is affiliated to Shri Baba Mastnath Math, Asthal Bohar.

Across the past decade, BMU has grown into a respected centre for professional and health-sciences education, offering programmes spanning Ayurveda, Pharmacy, Management, Law, Engineering, Nursing, Sciences, Humanities and Commerce.
